About This Initiative

The Algorithmic Transparency Project is a global movement demanding that social media platforms reveal how their recommendation algorithms work, who they suppress, and how collective action is being algorithmically mediated. We're building the tools and frameworks needed to audit AI systems through teleological, epistemological, and ontological analysis.

This initiative was inspired by research showing that current social media architectures inherently create hierarchy, tribalism, and elite gatekeeping - regardless of algorithm design. We've discovered that the problem isn't individual bad actors, but the architecture of amplification itself.

Our goal is to make algorithmic control visible and therefore contestable, enabling genuine collective action in digital spaces.
